Study Timetable maker also known as schedule planner is an online tool that helps you create your own timetable for study purpose. You can create a new timetable by clicking on each cell and specify the subject that you want to study at that time. To save the Timetable click the save button and it will be saved in your computer browser. To reset and start again click reset button. You can even print this timetable by clicking print button at the top. In order to access the saved timetable you do not have to do anything but visit the same URL and time table page again. Einsty does not store your any information on it's server all the information saved in this time table is saved in your PC itself.
